## Overview

**Introduction and Purpose:** This Standard establishes the process for identifying, assessing, and implementing regulatory changes that affect Afterpay US Inc.'s products and operations.

**Scope:** Applies to Afterpay US Services and its US-regulated activities.

**Definitions:**

| Terminology | Definition |
|---|---|
| **Regulatory Change** | Any new or amended law, regulation, rule, or guidance issued by a regulatory authority that may affect Afterpay US operations |
| **Staff Member(s)** | An employee or external worker hired by the Company who supports the operations covered by this Standard |

---

## [Brand/Entity]-Specific Requirements

### Afterpay

All regulatory change management activities specific to Afterpay US Inc.'s buy-now-pay-later (BNPL) products and state lending license obligations are governed by this Standard.

---

## Compliance & Enforcement

### Roles and Responsibilities

**Block, Inc. Roles:**

- **Regulatory Affairs Team:** Monitors federal regulatory developments; coordinates cross-entity impact assessments
- **Chief Compliance Officer:** Final escalation for material regulatory changes

**Subsidiary Roles:**

- **Afterpay US Compliance Lead:** Primary owner of this Standard; manages regulatory change register for Afterpay US
- **Legal Team:** Reviews regulatory changes for legal obligations; advises on implementation requirements

### Monitoring and Reporting

On an ongoing basis, the Standard Owner monitors regulatory publications, agency guidance, and legislative developments. Quarterly regulatory change reports are provided to Afterpay US leadership.

### Record Retention

All documents are retained according to the longer of Block's Data Classification & Handling Policy or applicable regulatory requirements.

### Exceptions

Exception requests must be documented in writing and submitted to the Policy Governance Team.
