## Overview

**Introduction and Purpose:** This Code of Business Conduct & Ethics establishes Block, Inc.'s standards for ethical conduct, legal compliance, and professional behavior for all employees and external workers.

**Scope:** Applies to all Block, Inc. employees, contractors, and affiliates globally.

**Definitions:**

| Terminology | Definition |
|---|---|
| **Staff Member(s)** | An employee or external worker (e.g., contingent labor, contractors) hired by the Company |
| **Conflict of Interest** | A situation where personal interests could improperly influence professional judgment |

---

## Compliance & Enforcement

### Roles and Responsibilities

**Block, Inc. Roles:**

- **Chief Compliance Officer (CCO):** Policy Owner; oversees Code of Ethics program
- **People Operations:** Administers training and attestation programs

**Subsidiary Roles:**

- **Subsidiary Leads:** Ensure local compliance with Code of Ethics requirements

### Monitoring and Reporting

On an ongoing basis, the Policy Owner monitors activities for compliance. Compliance provides regular updates to the Block, Inc. Board and executive management.

### Training

All employees complete annual Code of Ethics training as part of the Mandatory Training Program.

### Record Retention

All documents are retained according to the longer of Block's Data Classification & Handling Policy or applicable regulatory requirements.

### Exceptions

Exceptions to this Policy require CCO approval. Exception requests must be documented in writing and submitted to the Policy Governance Team.

### Confidentiality

This document is confidential and should only be accessed by employees.
