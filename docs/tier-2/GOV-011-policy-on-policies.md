## Overview

**Introduction and Purpose:** This Policy on Policies (PoP) governs the creation, review, approval, and retirement of all compliance documents across Block, Inc. and its subsidiaries. It establishes the document taxonomy, approval matrix, and change management requirements.

**Scope:** Applies to all In-Scope Documents (Policies, Frameworks, Standards, Enterprise Procedures) governed by the Block Compliance function.

**Definitions:**

| Terminology | Definition |
|---|---|
| **In-Scope Document** | A Policy, Framework, Standard, or Enterprise Procedure subject to the governance requirements in this PoP |
| **Document Owner** | Individual accountable for a document's accuracy, review cadence, and exception approvals |
| **Document Submitter** | Individual authorized to draft and submit changes to a document via the change management workflow |
| **Immaterial Change** | A change that does not substantively alter requirements, scope, or obligations |
| **Material Change** | A change that substantively alters requirements, scope, or obligations |

## Compliance & Enforcement

### Roles and Responsibilities

**Block, Inc. Roles:**

- **Policy Governance Team:** Administers the document management system; performs QC on all document changes; maintains document-registry.yaml
- **Document Owners:** Accountable for document accuracy, review cadence, and exception approvals
- **Document Submitters:** Authorized to draft and submit changes

**Subsidiary Roles:**

- **Subsidiary Compliance Leads:** Ensure subsidiary-specific requirements are reflected in applicable documents

### Monitoring and Reporting

The Policy Governance Team monitors review dates, exception expiry, and workflow compliance. Monthly digest reports are provided to Compliance Leadership.

### Record Retention

All documents are retained according to the longer of Block's Data Classification & Handling Policy or applicable regulatory requirements.

### Exceptions

Exceptions to requirements in this Policy require Committee approval. Exception requests must be submitted to the Policy Governance Team.
