## How to read this file

Each entry below represents one In-Scope Document. It provides:
- The document's current GitHub path (for migrated docs)
- A link to its LogicGate record (cross-reference during dual-system operation)
- Links to its published PDF and pre-migration history in Google Drive
- The signed approval record for its most recent pre-GitHub approval
- Migration and history notes

`[placeholder]` links indicate fields not yet populated for the PoC. Replace with real
Drive and LogicGate URLs when populating for Phase 2 migration.

## Pre-LogicGate history note

LogicGate was implemented in October 2025. Documents with effective dates prior to October 2025
have approval and change history predating LogicGate. That history is held in Google Drive
(PDF versions, signed committee/board minutes) and in the legacy document management system
used prior to LogicGate. Links to pre-LogicGate history are included in the Drive folder links
above where available.

For documents with no accessible pre-LogicGate history, the `Notes` field records the known
effective date and the reason for the gap. The absence of pre-GitHub history does not affect
the document's validity or the GitHub governance workflow going forward.
